About Me

Civil engineer turned performance marketer

I’m a digital marketing professional with 4+ years of experience managing high-value campaigns across Meta Ads, Google Ads and programmatic channels. Currently at Havas Media, I oversee a ₹5 crore monthly ad portfolio spanning quick commerce and NBFC clients.

My strength lies at the intersection of creativity and data — building campaigns that aren’t just visible, but measurable, scalable and consistently profitable. I thrive on turning complex data into clear, actionable strategy.

How I got here — During the 2019 lockdown I started a home business and ran my own Instagram and Facebook ads. The results fascinated me far more than the product itself. I dove deep into courses, certifications and real experiments — eventually making the full switch from civil engineering to digital marketing. Systems thinking meets human behaviour, and I wouldn’t have it any other way.
